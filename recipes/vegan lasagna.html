Vegan Lasagna

If you would like to be even healthier and aviod the rush hour of traffic that is clogged arteries from cholesterol, then this is the choice for you!

what a neet picture

Ingredients

  • boiled lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • sliced zucchini
  • vegan riccotta cheese
  • vegan shredded mozarella cheese
  • sundried tomatoes

directions

  1. lay down a layer of sauce with a brush
  2. lay down 4 to 5 lasagna noodles
  3. spread another layer of tomato sauce
  4. lay down a layer of zucchini slices
  5. add a layer of vegan riccotta cheese
  6. repeat steps 1 through 5 four more times
  7. finally top with vegan mozarella cheese and sundried tomatoes.
  8. bake at 350 degrees F for 60 minutes
