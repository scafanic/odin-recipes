vegetarian lasagna

this vegetarian lasagna is the perfect meal for a cold autumn evening.

This vegetarian lasagna is so tasty.

Ingredients

  • boiled lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • tomato paste
  • sliced zucchini
  • riccotta cheese
  • shredded mozarella cheese
  • sundried tomatoes

directions

  1. lay down a layer of sauce with a brush
  2. lay down 4 to 5 lasagna noodles
  3. spread another layer of tomato sauce
  4. lay down a layer of zucchini slices
  5. add a layer of riccotta cheese
  6. repeat steps 1 through 5 four mor times
  7. finally top with mozarella cheese and sundried tomatoes.
  8. bake at 350 degrees F for 60 minutes
